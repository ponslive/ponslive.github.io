My Story

Twenty years, one continuous thread.

Born and raised in India, I began my engineering journey in Coimbatore studying Computer Science at Karunya Institute of Technology. After graduating in 2006, I joined Infosys and quickly found myself working with Fortune 500 companies — Nike, Comcast, Baker Hughes, AOL — across the US. I never looked back.


Over 20 years, I've gone deep — from protocol conformance and device diagnostics to defining AI strategy for one of America's largest carriers. Today I live in New Jersey with my family, still pushing forward, still building things that matter.

Family

The anchor that keeps everything grounded.

My family is everything. Monisha, my wife, has been my steadiest supporter through every late-night architecture review, every product launch, every pivot. She keeps the world turning at home so I can keep building at work.


Our son Thugil Iniyan, born June 11, 2024, has already changed how I think about everything I build. Every platform, every system, every product decision — I now think about the world he'll grow up in. It's the best kind of motivation.


There's a clarity that comes from stepping away from a complex system design and just being present at home. It recalibrates everything. It reminds me that technology is a means, not an end.
